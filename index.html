Dr inż. Bartłomiej Nowak

Welcome! I'm PhD in Physics. My interdisciplinary work involves combining theoretical physics with social sciences. I use methods from non-linear dynamics, non-equilibrium processes, phase transitions and widely understood numerical techniques to study complex systems. My primary focus in this area is on modeling and analyzing socio-economic phenomena such as opinion dynamics, the diffusion of innovations, and group formations. In the future, I hope to improve my skills in programming and data analysis, but ultimately, my goal is to tackle interesting and challenging problems.

Research Interest

  • Agent-based modeling
  • Complex Systems
  • Phase Transitions
  • Statistical Physics
